The game has 2 players, playing in rounds

 

- In each turn, a player rolls a "dice", with the value represented by the fingers of the artists. The player rolls it many times as he whishes. Each result get added to his ROUND score

- BUT, if the player rolls a 6 (Paul McCartney), all his ROUND score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn

- The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his ROUND score gets added to his GLOBAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn

- The first player to reach 100 points on GLOBAL score wins the game
